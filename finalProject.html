Overwatch

Overwatch is a fast paced first person shooter game made in 2016 by the game developers "Blizzard". Blizzard has created many games over the years, though people say that Overwatch is the best one yet.

Overwatch contains a vast amount of different maps and a huge selection of different characters to play. Every charcter is different in it's own unique way, which is probaly why people have so much passing for this game.

Over the years, Overwatch has gathered many fans from around the world and has hosted some of the worlds biggest video game competitions.

Ark: Survival Evolved

Ark Survival is an open world multiplayer game containing various biomes, hundreds of different dinosaurs and unreal graphics.

The whole point of the game is to survive and "evolve" to tame and control the dinosaurs. The more you tame the more powerful you become

The cool thing about taming dinos is using them to farm resourses, kill bosses and protect your base from other players.
